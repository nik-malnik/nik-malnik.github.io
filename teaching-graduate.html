Digital Marketing and Social Media Strategy

Graduate · Tepper School of Business, Carnegie Mellon University

Overview

This course teaches students to develop comprehensive digital marketing strategies across multiple channels including search, social media, influencers, e-commerce, display advertising, and viral video content. The curriculum incorporates emerging technologies such as AI, AR/VR, and blockchain applications in marketing contexts.

Topics
  • Search engine marketing — PPC and SEO
  • Display and video advertising
  • Social media and influencer marketing
  • E-commerce platform optimization
  • Predictive and generative AI applications
  • Personalization, targeting, and privacy
  • Augmented and virtual reality in marketing
  • Web 3.0 — cryptocurrencies and NFTs
Case Studies

Real-world cases from Harvard Business School examining companies such as Air France (search marketing optimization), Squatty Potty (viral campaigns), and AirFox (blockchain applications).

Tools & Certifications

Students work with no-code and low-code platforms including Google Recommendations AI and Alteryx, and pursue certifications through HubSpot Academy and Google Skill Shop in digital advertising, social media, and analytics.
